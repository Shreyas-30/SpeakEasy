# SpeakEasy Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 7, 2026

SpeakEasy is an English-learning mobile application that helps users practice reading, vocabulary, listening, and speaking. This Privacy Policy explains what information SpeakEasy may process, how it is used, and the choices available to users.

## Information We Process

SpeakEasy may process the following categories of information:

- App usage data, such as selected topics, saved vocabulary, liked articles, and in-app preferences
- Content entered or selected in the app, such as words chosen for lookup, vocabulary items, and article interactions
- Audio-related content, if you use listening or speaking features
- Technical information required for the app to function, such as device-generated network requests and service responses

SpeakEasy does not currently require user account registration to use the app.

## How Information Is Used

SpeakEasy uses information to:

- personalize the reading feed
- provide vocabulary definitions and translations
- generate article and word audio playback
- support speaking and AI-assisted language-learning features
- improve app stability and feature quality

## Third-Party Services

SpeakEasy relies on third-party services to power parts of the product. Depending on the feature being used, limited information may be sent to these services for processing:

- OpenAI, for language-processing features such as translated vocabulary explanations
- ElevenLabs, for cloud text-to-speech features when enabled
- dictionaryapi.dev, for English word definitions
- News content providers such as The Guardian and GNews, for article retrieval
- Expo and Apple infrastructure, as needed to deliver and run the app

These providers process data according to their own privacy policies and terms.

## Audio and AI Features

If you use audio or AI-supported features, text and other relevant request content may be sent to the backend service used by SpeakEasy and then to third-party AI providers in order to:

- generate speech audio
- translate vocabulary definitions
- support future discussion and speaking-practice features

SpeakEasy is designed to send only the information needed to fulfill the requested feature.

## Data Storage

Some app data may be stored locally on your device, such as saved vocabulary, preferences, and selected topics.

Some requests may also be temporarily processed or cached on backend infrastructure in order to support app performance and feature delivery.

## Data Sharing

SpeakEasy does not sell personal information.

Information is only shared with service providers as necessary to operate app features, comply with legal obligations, or protect the security and integrity of the service.

## Children’s Privacy

SpeakEasy is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Your Choices

You may choose not to use optional features such as audio, translation, or future speaking features.

You may also remove locally stored items such as saved vocabulary or liked content from within the app where supported.

## Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If changes are made, the updated version will be posted at this location with a revised “Last updated” date.
